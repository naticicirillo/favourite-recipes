Chicken Alfredo Pasta

Description

This creamy and indulgent Chicken Alfredo Pasta is a perfect comfort dish that combines tender slices of chicken with fettuccine noodles bathed in a rich Alfredo sauce. The sauce, made with heavy cream, Parmesan cheese, and garlic, creates a velvety texture that coats the pasta beautifully. Garnished with fresh parsley, this dish is not only satisfying but also a delightful treat for pasta lovers.

Ingredients

  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 1 lb chicken breasts, sliced
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the fettuccine pasta according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. In a large skillet, melt butter over medium heat. Add sliced chicken and cook until browned and cooked through.
  3. Add minced garlic to the skillet and saut√© for 1-2 minutes.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a simmer. Stir in Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Toss in the cooked pasta and coat it with the Alfredo sauce.
  6. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
