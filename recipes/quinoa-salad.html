Quinoa Salad

Description

A refreshing and nutritious choice, the Vegetarian Quinoa Salad is a vibrant medley of flavors and textures. Nutty quinoa serves as the base, complemented by juicy cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumber, red onion, and crumbled feta cheese. The salad is dressed with a zesty combination of olive oil and lemon juice, making it a perfect light meal or a delightful side dish. Fresh basil adds a fragrant finish, enhancing the overall appeal.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup quinoa, rinsed
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 red onion, finely chopped
  • 1/4 cup feta cheese, crumbled
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 lemon, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh basil for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook quinoa in water according to package instructions. Let it cool.
  2. In a large bowl, combine quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, and feta cheese.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  4. Pour the dressing over the quinoa mixture and toss to combine.
  5. Garnish with fresh basil and refrigerate before serving.
